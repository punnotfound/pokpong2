Welcome to My Bangkok Adventures

Walking through the heart of Bangkok, I discover stories in every street and serenity in every temple. Join me as I explore the timeless beauty and culture of this magnificent city.

About My Hobby

Hi, I'm Pokpong. One of my greatest joys is wandering through Bangkok, exploring hidden corners, lively markets, and serene temples. Every walk is a chance to witness the city's vibrant life and peaceful sanctuaries.

Visiting temples allows me to experience Bangkok's spiritual heart, marvel at intricate architecture, and reflect amidst calm surroundings. Each temple tells a story that connects the past with the present.

Did You See?

Beyond the main streets, Bangkok hides secret alleys with tiny shrines, charming cafes, and murals that few tourists notice. Every stroll holds a discovery waiting to be found.

The Grand Palace

Hidden Gem

The Grand Palace

The Grand Palace is Bangkok's most iconic landmark, adorned with gold and rich history, and home to the revered Emerald Buddha.

Wat Pho

Famous for its Reclining Buddha, Wat Pho is a peaceful sanctuary, rich in Thai massage history and traditional learning.

Sao Chingcha

The Giant Swing is a historic symbol in Bangkok, once used in Brahmin ceremonies, now a visual link to the city's rich cultural traditions.

Bangkok City Library

A modern, quiet escape in the Old Town, combining contemporary design with calm spaces, perfect for reading or reflecting during your walk.
